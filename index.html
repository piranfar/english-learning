FluentBridge AI

Local-first AI English learning app for B1 → B2 Academic English and TOEFL readiness.

FluentBridge AI is a Django + React learning platform designed to help English learners improve grammar, vocabulary, reading, writing, speaking, shadowing, listening, and mistake review through structured AI-guided practice.

  • B1 → B2 Academic English / TOEFL 80+ roadmap
  • Speaking Coach with TOEFL-style feedback design
  • Writing Coach for practice, editing, paraphrasing, and sentence building
  • Reading Coach with generated passages, questions, scoring, and explanations
  • Vocabulary review and mistake clinic
  • Local-first architecture using Django, React, and Vite
